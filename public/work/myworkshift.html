Head of Product

MyWorkShift

In 2009, I founded MyWorkShift, an app for scheduling employees. As Head of Product, I produced all of the UX designs and managed the UI process along with software development. MyWorkShift began as a web application and gained a user base of 20,000 employees among 5,000 companies within the first year after launching.

Tutorials

MyWorkShift was designed to boil down the daunting task of creating a work schedule down to a few clicks. At that time, this was cutting-edge UX.
