Website

User Dynamics, LLC. is my freelance UX agency. This is the website concept. My starting point was the logo; I wanted this to clearly communicate the company's expertise. Going off the them of "dynamics", I designed three-dimensional objects that would move into place to make the combined shape of a "U" and a "D". From there, I took the same concepts of motion, and applied them to a progressive story that unfolds as the user navigates the site.
