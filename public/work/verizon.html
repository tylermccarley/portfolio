One Talk

One Talk® is an extension of your business phone line to all your personal devices. Built for teams on the go, it propogates phone calls, video calls, and SMS text messages to multiple devices through any carrier or Wi-Fi network.

Designed for iOS and Android (phones and tablets), as well as Mac OS and Windows, my team designed over 1,500 screens. I personally led the UX, UI, and XD for Mac OS and Windows, played a heavy role in the Android UX, and translated the Android app to a UX and UI that was conducive to Apple's environment— the goal being to create an experience that was consistent across multiple platforms yet readily understandable within each unique platform.

The end product? One Line. One Experience. One Talk®.
